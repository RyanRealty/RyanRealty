## 1. Instagram Feed (single post)

### A. 2026 algorithm details

Instagram Feed in 2026 uses a ranking model that weighs saves and shares as the strongest engagement signals, with comments third and likes a distant fourth (Buffer 9.6M-post analysis, January 2026). A save from a non-follower carries roughly 3x the distribution weight of a like from a follower. The Feed algorithm applies a "reachability score" based on account health: posting frequency 4+ times per week raises the score; deleting posts repeatedly lowers it. Non-follower reach begins after the account demonstrates a 60%+ three-second hold rate across at least 12 recent posts. The recency window for Feed ranking is approximately 48 hours, after which older posts see diminishing distribution to non-followers.

### B. Exact format spec

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Aspect ratio | 4:5 (vertical) preferred; 1:1 acceptable; 1.91:1 (landscape) is penalized |
| Image resolution | 1080x1350 px at 4:5 |
| Caption limit | 2,200 characters (display truncates at ~125 chars before "more") |
| First-comment limit | 2,200 characters |
| Bio character limit | 150 characters |
| Hashtag limit | Up to 30; 3-5 performs best in 2026 per Creator Academy guidance |
| File size | JPEG under 8 MB; PNG under 8 MB |
| Supported codecs | n/a (static image surface) |
| Cover image spec | The static image itself; no separate cover |
| Safe zone | 90 px margin each edge for critical text |

### C. Hashtag strategy, posting cadence, best times (Mountain Time)

Use 3-5 hashtags. One broad (#realestate), one Oregon-niche (#oregonrealestate or #bendoregon), one subdivision or neighborhood (#nwcrossingbend, #tereboworegon), and one owned brand tag (#ryanrealtybend). Never tag the architect, builder, or staging company; it reads as paid promotion and invites algorithm suppression. Best posting times in Mountain Time: Tuesday and Wednesday 9-10 AM, Thursday 11 AM-1 PM. Avoid Sunday and Monday for new-audience reach; reserve those days for Stories. Recommended cadence: 1 Feed post per week, paired with 2 Reels and daily Stories.

### D. What is banned or suppressed

Posts with external links in the caption body (links only function in bio). Posts that use follow-for-follow language ("follow me for more"). Auto-posting from third-party tools that do not use the official Instagram Graph API triggers a reach penalty. Reposted content from TikTok with visible TikTok watermark is suppressed. Mass hashtag walls (20+ hashtags in caption or first comment) flag as spam behavior. Content that includes text overlays covering more than 20% of image area may be flagged as an ad-type post and redirected to paid-only distribution.

### E. Real-estate compliance

NAR Clear Cooperation Policy (MLS Statement 8.0): any public Instagram post marketing a listing to a broader audience than the brokerage's own employees constitutes public marketing and triggers the requirement to enter the listing into the MLS within one business day. Do not post "coming soon" with address and price on Feed if the listing has not been submitted to COAR (Central Oregon Association of Realtors) MLS. Oregon Administrative Rule OAR 863-015-0215 requires that real estate advertising disclose the licensed brokerage name. Ryan Realty must appear in the bio or post if the post is advertising a specific property. Fair housing: do not use language that indicates a preference for buyers or sellers of a specific race, religion, national origin, sex, disability, or familial status (42 U.S.C. § 3604). Neighborhood descriptions must be factual and geographic, not demographic. "Family-friendly neighborhood" is permissible; "great for families with children" may be interpreted as familial-status preference depending on context.

### F. Verbatim caption examples

See platform-bible-captions.md §1-Instagram-Feed for 10+ verbatim examples with attribution and engagement data.

### G. Logo-is-a-closer rule

For static Feed images that are photos (listing exterior, property feature, landscape), no logo appears on the photo itself. If the image is a designed graphic (market stat card, carousel cover), the Ryan Realty wordmark may appear in the bottom 10% of the image frame only, at no more than 15% image width.

### H. What this means for Ryan Realty producers

Every Feed post gets one clear factual hook in the first 125 characters. The producer writes the caption so the first sentence stands alone as a complete thought. A listing Feed post leads with a specific detail about the property (the view, the acreage, the garage size) before any transactional language. Market stat posts lead with the number. The producer checks the first line against the banned-phrases list before writing the second line.

## 2. Instagram Reels

### A. 2026 algorithm details

Instagram Reels in 2026 ranks primarily on watch-through rate, with the 60%+ three-second hold rate as the hard floor for algorithmic expansion beyond followers (Meta Creator Academy, 2026). Below 40% hold, the Reel is suppressed and will not be shown to non-followers. Saves and shares are the next highest signals. Comments weighted for quality (word-count above 3) outperform single-emoji comments 4:1. Reels that use a trending audio track from the commercial library within 72 hours of the sound's emergence receive a temporary distribution boost. Reels posted natively via the Instagram app or via the official Graph API receive the same distribution weight; third-party post-scheduling via non-Graph-API tools see a 15-25% reach penalty. The Reels algorithm also applies a "topic clustering" model: consistent Reels about a single topic (Bend real estate) build a topical authority score that increases distribution over time.

### B. Exact format spec

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 (mandatory for full Reels experience) |
| Resolution | 1080x1920 px |
| Duration | 15-90s; sweet spot 30-45s for discovery |
| Caption limit | 2,200 characters; first 125 shown before "more" |
| Hook timing | Motion by frame 12 (0.4s); content text by frame 30 (1.0s) |
| Safe zone | 90 px each edge; bottom 250 px (UI overlay) is a dead zone |
| File size | Under 4 GB |
| Codec | H.264 or H.265; AAC audio; .mp4 container |
| Cover image | Custom thumbnail recommended; 1080x1920 JPEG |
| Caption text (burned in) | Geist 500, white, drop shadow; full-sentence active-word highlight |

### C. Hashtag strategy, posting cadence, best times (Mountain Time)

3-5 hashtags at bottom of caption, separate line. Geographic first (#bendoregon), then niche (#centraloregonahomes), then owned (#ryanrealtybend). Do not embed hashtags in the caption body mid-sentence. Best times in Mountain Time: Tuesday-Thursday 10 AM-12 PM for discovery reach; 5-7 PM for listing-reveal content targeting post-work house-hunters; 8-10 PM for widest non-follower Reel distribution. Posting cadence: 2 Reels per week consistently. Consistency outperforms burst; 2 per week for 8 weeks beats 16 in one week.

### D. What is banned or suppressed

TikTok watermark visible in frame triggers immediate suppression (Meta confirmed). Slow or static first frame (no motion at frame 0) drops three-second hold rate by 30-40%. Agent logo or brokerage name in the first two seconds signals promotional content, reducing non-follower distribution. Links in caption body do not function and train the algorithm to treat the account as link-posting. Aspect ratios other than 9:16 receive black letterbox bars, which signal lower production quality and reduce swipe-through rate.

### E. Real-estate compliance

Same MLS submission rule as Feed: any Reel that publicly markets a specific property requires the listing to be in the COAR MLS within one business day. A Reel showing a property interior or exterior with price constitutes public marketing. For fair housing: narrated Reel VO must not include neighborhood descriptions referencing protected classes. Oregon license disclosure: Ryan Realty name must be identifiable through the account bio or end-card, not necessarily in every Reel frame (OREA guidance 2024, still operative 2026).

### F. Verbatim caption examples

See platform-bible-captions.md §2-Instagram-Reels for 10+ verbatim examples with attribution and engagement data.

### G. Logo-is-a-closer rule

Zero in-frame logo during the Reel. Brand identity is built through the consistent navy/cream color system, Matt's voice and face, and the @ryanrealtybend handle visible in the account UI. The end card (final 2-3 seconds) may include the stacked_logo_white.png on a navy band, one CTA line, and the handle. This is the logo's earned placement.

### H. What this means for Ryan Realty producers

Every Reel the producer renders must have motion at frame 0, content text by frame 30, and a specific hook element (price, place name, contradicting claim, or visual surprise) before second 2. The producer runs a blackdetect check on the first 12 frames before submit. The producer does not use trending audio in the render itself (ElevenLabs VO is the audio track), but may request that the social publisher overlays a trending audio track at -18 LUFS when uploading via the Graph API's audio-mix option.

## 3. Instagram Stories

### A. 2026 algorithm details

Stories in 2026 operate on a separate ranking model from Feed and Reels. The primary signal is direct message replies from viewers. Polls, question stickers, and quiz stickers generate native engagement signals that the algorithm treats as high-quality interactive events. Accounts that post Stories daily maintain higher "story affinity" with their followers, meaning their Stories appear near the front of the Story tray more consistently (Instagram Business Help, 2025). Story reach is largely limited to existing followers; non-follower Stories distribution is minimal unless a Story gets shared as a forward. Stories marked with a location tag or hashtag sticker are surfaced in location/hashtag Story aggregations, which can drive small organic discovery.

### B. Exact format spec

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 |
| Resolution | 1080x1920 |
| Duration | Up to 60s per Story card (15s auto-advance for video under 15s) |
| Caption/text limit | No caption field; all text is burned into the Story or added via IG native text tool |
| Safe zone | 250 px top (navigation bar); 250 px bottom (Story controls); 90 px each side |
| Sticker zone | Center 900x1200 px safe for interactive stickers |
| File size | Under 4 GB for video; under 30 MB for photo |
| Codec | H.264; AAC; .mp4 |
| Expiry | 24 hours; can save to Highlights permanently |

### C. Hashtag strategy, posting cadence, best times (Mountain Time)

Hashtag stickers in Stories index into the location/hashtag Story feed. Use 1-2 stickers maximum; more looks like spam. Stories are a daily format. Morning post (7-9 AM Mountain Time) catches the commute scroll; evening post (7-9 PM) reaches the post-dinner browser. Daily Stories are the single highest-frequency format in this playbook. Low production overhead is the point: phone-shot, real-time, authentic.

### D. What is banned or suppressed

Stories with third-party watermarks (TikTok, CapCut export watermarks) are not suppressed algorithmically but damage brand perception. Links via the "link sticker" require at least 10,000 followers or a verified account for some destinations (Instagram policy). Stories that are pure link cards with no visual content generate low tap-forward rates. Republishing the same Reel as a Story on the same day is a common but ineffective tactic; Story views for republished Reels average 40% lower than for original Story content.

### E. Real-estate compliance

A Story that includes a listing address and price constitutes a public marketing event, even though it expires in 24 hours. The MLS one-business-day rule applies to Story content the same as it does to Feed posts. Saved to Highlights means the content is permanently public. Fair housing rules apply to all Story text overlays and VO.

### F. Verbatim caption examples

See platform-bible-captions.md §3-Instagram-Stories for 10+ verbatim examples with attribution and engagement data.

### G. Logo-is-a-closer rule

The profile photo (Ryan Realty Jax mascot or Matt headshot) is visible in the Story tray as the brand signal. No logo is burned into Story frames. End-Story slides that are designed graphics (market data, just-listed, just-closed) may include the wordmark in the bottom 15% of the frame.

### H. What this means for Ryan Realty producers

Stories are low-production, high-frequency. Producers do not run the full Remotion render pipeline for Stories. Phone-shot video, native IG text overlays, polls, and questions are the primary format. The producer's job for Stories is to supply a daily content calendar item: the specific question, poll topic, or local fact to post. The content engine marks one Story item per day in the action-row queue.

## 4. Instagram Carousel

### A. 2026 algorithm details

Carousels in 2026 have a unique algorithmic property: Instagram re-serves the carousel to accounts that saw it but did not swipe through all slides. This creates a second distribution window, typically 24-48 hours after initial post, that can double effective reach (Instagram Business Help, 2025; TrueFuture Media 2026 creator analysis). The algorithm treats saves as the primary engagement signal for Carousels, followed by swipe-through completion rate. A Carousel that gets 500 saves beats a Reel with 5,000 likes in distribution weight. The first slide functions as a standalone thumbnail in the Feed; it must hook without requiring the viewer to swipe. Carousels with 7-10 slides outperform shorter carousels in reach, because partial-completion creates re-serve events.

### B. Exact format spec

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Aspect ratio | 4:5 (portrait, recommended) or 1:1 (square) |
| Resolution per slide | 1080x1350 px at 4:5 |
| Slides | 2-10 slides per carousel |
| Caption limit | 2,200 characters |
| Font (slide text) | Amboqia Boriango for headlines; Geist for body |
| Colors | Navy #102742 on cream #faf8f4 (heritage register) |
| Safe zone | 90 px each edge on every slide |
| File size per slide | Under 30 MB per image |
| Logo | Final slide only, bottom-right, max 15% slide width |

### C. Hashtag strategy, posting cadence, best times (Mountain Time)

Same hashtag spec as Feed: 3-5 tags, geographic-first. Post Carousels on Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM-12 PM Mountain Time. Cadence: 1-2 Carousels per week. Carousels serve as the primary market-data format on IG, so the weekly market stat drop maps to a Carousel. The Carousel caption teases the payoff without giving away all slides: "Here's what sold in Bend last week. Swipe to see the number that surprised me."

### D. What is banned or suppressed

Carousels with the first slide being pure text on a solid color (no image) underperform against photo-first first slides. Video slides mixed with image slides in the same Carousel must have matching 4:5 aspect ratio or IG letterboxes the video. Using the same image twice in a Carousel is a production error the algorithm does not penalize, but it kills viewer trust.

### E. Real-estate compliance

Carousels showing property photos with address and price follow the same MLS one-business-day rule. Market data carousels must use sourced statistics. Every number shown in a Carousel slide must trace to a verified source (Supabase `market_stats_cache`, MLS pull, or named agency). Slide text that includes a neighborhood name must not use demographic descriptors.

### F. Verbatim caption examples

See platform-bible-captions.md §4-Instagram-Carousel for 10+ verbatim examples with attribution and engagement data.

### G. Logo-is-a-closer rule

First slide: no logo. Slides 2-9: no logo. Final slide: logo-blue.png bottom-right at 15% slide width. The final slide is the credential slide: Ryan Realty wordmark, ryan-realty.com, 541.213.6706.

### H. What this means for Ryan Realty producers

The list-kit producer generates Carousels as a primary output format. The generator uses the approved Python scripts in `scripts/build_list_kit_carousel.py`. Do not reimplement layout from scratch. Adapt source data only. The final slide always contains the wordmark, web, and phone. Every number on every slide gets a citation entry in the accompanying `citations.json`.

## 5. Facebook Feed

### A. 2026 algorithm details

Facebook Feed in 2026 runs a "meaningful social interactions" ranking model that weights comments (especially comment threads) and shares above reactions. Posts that generate back-and-forth conversation in comments receive disproportionate distribution. The October 2025 algorithm update deprioritized link posts; posts with a link in the body see approximately 40% lower organic reach than posts without links (Meta transparency report, Q4 2025). Facebook's demographic skew toward 35-65+ makes it the primary organic channel for the move-up and retirement buyer segment active in Bend. 4:5 vertical images take 33% more screen real estate on mobile and receive 15-20% higher engagement rates than square or landscape posts.

### B. Exact format spec

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Aspect ratio | 4:5 preferred; 1:1 acceptable |
| Image resolution | 1080x1350 px |
| Caption limit | 63,206 characters (display truncates at ~480 chars before "See more") |
| First-comment limit | 8,000 characters |
| Hashtag limit | 3-5; same as IG |
| Video for Feed | Up to 240 min; MP4 H.264 |
| Safe zone | 90 px each edge |
| Cover image | n/a for standard post |

### C. Hashtag strategy, posting cadence, best times (Mountain Time)

3-5 hashtags, bottom of caption. Geographic-first. Best times in Mountain Time: Tuesday-Thursday 10 AM-12 PM; Wednesday 3-5 PM secondary window. Total Facebook posting cadence: 5 posts per week (2 Reels, 2 Feed image/carousel, 1 text-only or mixed). Text-only posts that contain a genuine local insight perform surprisingly well with the 45-65 demographic, who read more of the caption than younger audiences. Links go in the first comment, never in the post body.

### D. What is banned or suppressed

Link in post body: 40% reach penalty. Reposted TikTok with watermark: suppressed. Posts that explicitly ask for reactions or shares ("React if you agree") violate Facebook engagement-bait policies and may result in account reach reduction. Generic stock photos trigger a lower distribution score than authentic property photos or photos from Central Oregon.

### E. Real-estate compliance

Same NAR Clear Cooperation and fair-housing rules as IG. Facebook's ad policies additionally prohibit targeting of housing ads based on zip code as a proxy for race. Facebook's Special Ad Category for Housing must be applied to any paid post advertising a specific property or asking for real estate leads. Organic posts are not subject to Special Ad Category rules. Oregon license disclosure: brokerage name in bio or post.

### F. Verbatim caption examples

See platform-bible-captions.md §5-Facebook-Feed for 10+ verbatim examples with attribution and engagement data.

### G. Logo-is-a-closer rule

Facebook Feed's older demographic is more logo-tolerant than short-form platforms. For organic Feed posts, the logo appears in the designed graphic element (market stat card, carousel first slide) in the footer band. For pure photo posts, no logo on the photo. Profile page header image is the primary brand real estate.

### H. What this means for Ryan Realty producers

Facebook Feed posts are not a separate production effort. Every IG Feed post gets cross-published to Facebook Feed via the Graph API (the publish route at `app/api/social/publish/route.ts` handles both simultaneously). Caption adapts: Facebook tolerates slightly longer captions (up to 150 chars before truncation is less critical here), and the link goes in the first comment automatically.

## 6. Facebook Reels

Facebook Reels in 2026 receives the same-day upload bonus: native Reels uploaded the day of filming receive 50% more distribution than content clearly re-uploaded from an archive (Meta Creator Academy, October 2025 update). The completion-rate floor for non-follower distribution is 60% at 15 seconds. Facebook Reels skew toward the 35-55 demographic and tolerate more visible branding than TikTok or IG Reels. An agent name + brokerage in the lower-third does not penalize reach on Facebook the way it does on the younger platforms. The primary use case is cross-publishing IG Reels to FB Reels on the same day.

### D. What is banned or suppressed

### E. Real-estate compliance

### F. Verbatim caption examples

## 8. Facebook Groups

### A. 2026 algorithm details

Facebook Groups in 2026 are the highest-organic-reach Facebook surface available to real estate professionals who are not running paid ads. Group posts are not subject to the same link-suppression rules as Page posts; links in Group posts receive normal organic reach. Group content appears in members' Feeds directly when groups are set to "Highlight posts to group members." Facebook's algorithm rewards groups that maintain high activity (daily posts, active comment threads) by promoting them to potential new members via "Suggested groups." A local group of 500-2,000 active members in the Bend/Central Oregon market generates more qualified seller leads per month than equivalent Page reach (Laura Griffin model; documented in `docs/FACEBOOK_SELLER_GROWTH_PIPELINE.md`).

### B. Exact format spec

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Post format | All standard Facebook post types supported |
| Video | Same spec as Feed video; native upload recommended |
| Image | Same spec as Feed image |
| Caption | 63,206 character limit |
| Group size to switch from public to private | 500 members |
| Optimal posting frequency | Daily for sustained growth |

### C. Hashtag strategy, posting cadence, best times (Mountain Time)

Hashtags have minimal effect inside Groups; the Group itself is the discovery mechanism. Post daily. Best times in Mountain Time: 8-10 AM and 7-9 PM. Mix content types: 90% local-value (neighborhood info, market data, local events, DIY home tips), 10% real estate service content. Do not turn the Group into a listings feed.

### D. What is banned or suppressed

Groups with a spam reputation (too many listing posts, promotional language) get deprioritized by the recommendation algorithm for new-member acquisition. Selling directly to members without adding value first results in removal and ban by group admins (self-owned group: Matt is the admin, but the principle still applies for member retention).

### E. Real-estate compliance

No specific MLS implications for Group discussion posts. If a Group post advertises a specific listing with price and address as a public marketing event, the MLS one-business-day rule applies. Fair housing: Group rules and post moderation must not discriminate based on protected class. The Group description and welcome post should include a fair-housing disclaimer.

### F. Verbatim caption examples

### G. Logo-is-a-closer rule

Group cover photo may include the Ryan Realty wordmark. Group posts follow Feed rules: designed graphics may include logo in footer band; photo posts do not.

### H. What this means for Ryan Realty producers

The content engine does not auto-post to Groups; Group content is Matt-authored or follows a pre-approved value-content calendar. The brain can queue Group post suggestions in action rows but does not auto-publish to Groups.

## 9. Facebook Marketplace

### A. 2026 algorithm details

Facebook Marketplace in 2026 includes a Homes for Sale section that functions as a listing syndication surface. Marketplace listings appear in both the Marketplace tab and in Feed for users who have browsed similar real estate. The algorithm surfaces Marketplace listings by geographic proximity and price range. Marketplace is not a primary lead-gen channel for listing-side business; it captures buyer inquiries. Listing updates (price changes) refresh the listing's position in search results.

### B. Exact format spec

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Listing category | Homes for Sale |
| Photos | Up to 99; JPEG; each under 30 MB |
| Description limit | 5,000 characters |
| Price | Required; enter as the list price |
| Location | Property address required |
| Bedrooms / bathrooms | Required fields |

### C. Hashtag strategy, posting cadence, best times (Mountain Time)

Hashtags are not used in Marketplace listings. Listings stay active until sold, expired (after 7 days they prompt renewal), or manually removed. Best practice: renew or update the listing on Tuesdays and Thursdays to refresh position. Do not cross-post a Marketplace listing to the Page Feed manually; Meta handles cross-surface display automatically.

### D. What is banned or suppressed

Marketplace policies prohibit: advertising rental units with discriminatory language, using bait-and-switch pricing, listing a property that has already sold, or adding irrelevant keywords to the description to game search (Fair Housing Act considerations apply here specifically).

### E. Real-estate compliance

A Facebook Marketplace Homes for Sale listing constitutes public marketing. The MLS one-business-day submission rule applies. Oregon and NAR rules require that the listing brokerage be disclosed. The description must include Ryan Realty as the listing brokerage. Discriminatory content (restrictions on familial status, disability) is illegal and violates Facebook Terms of Service.

### F. Verbatim caption examples

## 21. Cross-cutting: Email

### Format and delivery

### Content spec

Subject line: 40-60 characters; specific local fact, not a teaser. Preheader: 80-100 characters supplementing the subject. Body: Geist body text, sentence case headings, no em-dashes, no semicolons. ### Compliance

CAN-SPAM Act (15 U.S.C. § 7701): every commercial email must include a physical mailing address, a functioning unsubscribe mechanism honored within 10 business days, and a non-deceptive subject line. Oregon does not have a separate email marketing law; federal CAN-SPAM applies. Fair housing: email list segmentation by protected class (race, religion, national origin, sex, disability, familial status) is illegal. Segmenting by buyer vs. seller intent is legal.

## 26. The logo-is-a-closer doctrine

Every short-form platform (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels) trains its algorithm to suppress content that signals "this is an ad." An in-frame brokerage logo in the first two seconds is the clearest possible ad signal. The algorithm interprets it as promotional content and reduces distribution to non-followers before any human viewer has made that judgment.

This is not a rule of thumb. It is documented platform behavior. TikTok's Content Posting API guidelines explicitly prohibit logos in video frames. Instagram's internal testing (cited in Meta Creator Academy 2026) shows that branded watermarks reduce non-follower Reel distribution by 15-25%. YouTube's CTR data shows logo-free thumbnails for real estate topics outperform logo-present thumbnails by 20-38% when a face is present.

The top real estate creators who have built the largest audiences have all reached the same conclusion through their own testing. Daniel Heider (3.7M TikTok followers), Glennda Baker (350K+ daily IG views), Madison Sutton (100% of business sourced from social), and Ryan Serhant (whose production team explicitly removed in-frame logos from Reels in 2024) all run zero persistent in-frame logos on short-form organic content.

The Chanel parallel is precise: over-exposure of a logo devalues it. A logo on every frame makes it an ad for a brand nobody asked to engage with. Restraint is the luxury signal. The logo earns its placement by appearing only where a viewer expects a credential: the end card, the thumbnail, the carousel's final slide, the printed flyer, the email header, the YouTube branding watermark (after 0:30), and the yard sign.

For Ryan Realty specifically: Matt's face + the Bend landscape + the navy/cream palette + a consistent voice, repeated across 30+ pieces of content, builds more brand equity than any watermark.

See `social_media_skills/platform-best-practices/SKILL.md` for the full per-surface logo placement matrix.
