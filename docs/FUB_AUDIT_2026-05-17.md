## 13. Findings + Recommendations

### Findings (the picture in 8 bullets)

1. **The CRM is dramatically over-tooled relative to actual usage.** 67 action plans, 12 smart lists, 669 email templates, 158 tags ‚Äî for a team of 3 brokers handling ~15 real seller leads per quarter. The vast majority of that surface is dormant Kunversion (KTS) imports.
2. **Only one seller-targeted action plan has ever fired** ‚Äî `Seller - Home Evaluation Request` (id 5, isUsed:true, 5 steps, 4 email touches over 7 days). It is the de facto seller workflow, and it's barely used.
3. **The 11 distinct seller-tag variants** (`Seller`, `Seller Lead`, `Seller Intent`, `hot-seller`, `audience:seller`, `source:seller-lp`, `auto:seller-seq:new`, `auto:seller-seq:watch`, `LP-Home-Value`, `nurture-only`, `Nurture Seller`) come from at least 6 different conventions. The brain compensates with case-insensitive matching; the FUB UI does not. Mistagged leads silently miss action plans.
4. **No webhooks. No custom smart lists. No custom lead sources via the API.** The CRM is entirely poll-driven and the segmentation surface is the FUB defaults.
5. **The custom fields described in `docs/FUB_CUSTOM_FIELDS.md` don't exist.** The 10 fields that DO exist are all MLS listing metadata (`customMLSNumber`, `customListingDaysOnMarket`, etc.) ‚Äî not lead-intel.
6. **No round-robin assignment is actually happening.** 24 of 26 recent leads are assigned to Matt. Rebecca got 2, Paul got 0.
7. **Nobody is being promoted out of `Lead` stage.** 10,768 people sit at `Lead`; only 1 person is in `A - Hot 1-3 Months`; 0 in `B - Warm 3-6 Months`; 47 in `C - Cold`; 8 are active clients; 21 are past clients. The funnel doesn't move.
8. **Three empty action plans** (id 65, 66, 67 ‚Äî `Expired Second Touch (Apr 2026)`, `Out of State Second Touch (Apr 2026)`, `Expired Listing SMS v2 (Apr 2026)`) are scaffolds Matt created in April but never populated. They show as Active in the UI but do nothing.
