Methodology

How we count

Every number on this page traces to a row in the Ryan Realty database, with the query, filter, and timestamp that produced it.
  • Live pulse — market_pulse_live where geo_type='city' and geo_slug='bend'. Methodology version v3-2026-05-07. Scope: PropertyType=A (single-family residence). Pulled 2026-05-15 17:30 UTC.
  • Months of supply = active_count ÷ (closed_last_6_months ÷ 6). Thresholds: ≤ 4 seller's market, 4–6 balanced, ≥ 6 buyer's market.
  • 12-month trend — market_stats_cache, period_type=monthly. Each point uses every SFR close in that calendar month.
  • Featured listings — top 12 active SFR by list price. Video badges on listings where has_virtual_tour=true in the MLS feed.
  • Neighborhood photos — geo-tagged via asset_library · upgrade to per-neighborhood originals planned.
  • Boundaries — City of Bend GIS Neighborhood Districts polygons + TIGER/Line 2024 city polygon (GEOID=4105800).
  • Hero video — Bend YTD 2026 market report rendered locally from the Spark + Supabase feeds (Remotion + ElevenLabs Victoria voice, locked 2026-04-27).
