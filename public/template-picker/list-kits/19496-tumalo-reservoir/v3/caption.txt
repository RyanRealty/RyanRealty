NEW LISTING | A Tumalo Retreat with Cascade Views

Set on 2.28 acres just twelve minutes from downtown Bend, this single-level Tumalo retreat captures what Central Oregon mountain living has always been about — Cascade views from nearly every room, a creek running through mature trees, and a rural pace of life within reach of everything.

This is Tumalo at its quietest, with downtown Bend, The Bite, and Mt. Bachelor all within an easy drive.
