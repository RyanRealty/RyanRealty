Pulse · video-first demo

Every listing card defaults to video

Eight real Central Oregon listings pulled from the live MLS feed. Seven have an embeddable property tour (Cloudflare Stream, Vimeo, YouTube, Aryeo, Riley Visuals). The closed sale animates the hero photo. One video plays at a time. Double-tap any card to like.

In-app browser sims show what users see when they tap our ad on Instagram. Video-feed sim shows the worst case if a producer repurposes the card frame as a 1080×1920 social video.
