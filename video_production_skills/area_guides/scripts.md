## VIDEO FORMAT (Same structure for every neighborhood)

## BRANDING SPECS

**Logo:** Ryan Realty navy circular badge (Canva asset MAGtznhi3T0)
**Colors:** Navy blue primary (#1B2B4B), White secondary
**Fonts in CapCut:** Use clean sans serif (closest to AzoSans), bold for headlines
**Text Style:** White text with subtle drop shadow for readability over footage
**Logo Placement:** Bottom right corner, small but visible throughout video
**CTA Text:** White on navy overlay or semi-transparent dark background

## EDITING NOTES

- Color correct all footage with warm cinematic LUT
- Use speed ramping on transitions (slow to fast to slow)
- Add subtle zoom (Ken Burns) on photo slides
- Cross dissolve transitions between clips (keep it clean, not gimmicky)
- Text animations: fade up from bottom, hold 3 to 4 seconds, fade out
- Background music at 30 to 40% volume
- No voiceover (music and text only for this batch)
- Export Reels at 1080x1920 (9:16) MP4
- Export YouTube at 1920x1080 (16:9) MP4
