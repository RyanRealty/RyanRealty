3. Outside the box — closest thing to “no licensing friction”

5b. Smart use in the listing-tour reel (once assets are truly Caldera-only)

  • Placement: short intercuts (2–3s) between MLS photo acts and neighborhood card — never as a fake “subject exterior” unless the frame is literally that listing.
